Commands:

  • !ping – Pong!
  • !hello – Greets you
  • !join – Bot joins your voice channel
  • !leave – Bot leaves voice channel
  • !play [YouTube link] – Plays a song
  • !skip – Skip current song
  • !pause – Pause song
  • !resume – Resume song
  • !queue – Show current queue
  • !nowplaying – Show currently playing song
